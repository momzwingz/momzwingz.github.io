My Journey

Hi! Welcome to MomZwingZ. This is Sahaya Divya. Engineer by profession now a busy homemaker juggling between a teenager and a kindergardener. My childhood has been colorful. I owe my thanks to my school Auxilium, along with studies it taught me the all round formation giving importance for co curricular and extra curricular activities. My inspiration is my mom who is a writer,lyrisict,actor,singer,orator and the best chemisty teacher! She has ruled our school stage for years,yes she was my chemistry teacher in my higher secondary. My school and college days went busy with studies,comeptitions and performances.

Had a great short journey professionally at Satyam computers and Tata consultancy services. It is after marriage I started exploring various forms of dance (other than bharathnatyam which i learnt during my childhood)like hip-hop, belly dance ,bollywood and was a Zumba co trainer too . I always feel one my boldest decisions was my second child with 8 years age differnce. Suffering from postpartum depressions I always had fear whether I could bounce back physically and mentally. It was after her, I have performed several dance shows, flash mobs,Chingay Parade and even bagged 'International dance championship 2020' conducted by Dance Plane, Singapore.

The common thing between my teenager and kindergardener is STORY. Both love to hear stories how ever ,what ever genre it is! I started reading books espcially Vizhiyan's books for kids,following his whatsapp story series and his blogs. I am produ to say that he is my school senior. I have also seen the works of my ex colleague Vithya Danaraj who is a professional story teller. Now my stepping stone as a story teller. Being an amateur singer I believe that song along with stories can impress and touch kids easily.

MomZwingZ is to empower each of the moms. Failiures, depressions, loneliness, helplessness can make you and me to suffer but let's fight against it .We are the best moms ! Let us come out with flying colours along with MomZwingZ!

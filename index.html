Why are Stories Important for Children?

Stories play a vital role in the growth and development of children. They are the means of transferring information, experience, attitude or point of view. Story sparks child’s imagination and stimulates curiosity. It is magic! They help children with their,

  • Confidence Levels
  • Language and Learning
  • Relaxation
  • Development of Imagination
  • Coping With Feelings
